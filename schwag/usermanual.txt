PAGE 2: CONTROLS (ARCADE STYLE)

In this FMV adventure, you do not move Dirk freely around the screen. Instead, you guide him through the movie by pressing the correct direction or the sword button at the precise moment.

The Inputs

Control Pad (D-Pad): Used for movement and directional reactions.

Up: Move Forward / Climb Up / Grab Top Rope

Down: Duck / Crouch / Climb Down / Back Away

Left: Move Left / Jump Left / Dodge Left

Right: Move Right / Jump Right / Dodge Right

Buttons (A, B, X, Y): All face buttons function as the SWORD.

Press any button to Swing Sword / Attack / Draw Weapon.

Start: Pause the game.

Select: Insert Coin / Reset (Depends on configuration).

PAGE 3: FMV CUE GUIDE & TIMING

The difference between life and death is a split second. This version runs on the original arcade logic.

How to Play

Watch the Scene: Dirk walks into a room or situation automatically via the MSU-1 video stream.

Wait for the Cue: A specific object will flash, or a danger will present itself.

React: Press the corresponding direction or Sword button immediately.

The "Flash" System
The game helps you by briefly flashing yellow on the object you need to interact with or the direction you need to go.

Yellow Flash on a Door/Path/Wall: Move in that direction (Up, Down, Left, or Right).

Yellow Flash on Dirk's Sword: Press the Sword button immediately.

Timing Your Moves

Do Not Spam: Mashing buttons will often cause a "Misstep" (death). Press the button once decisively when you see the cue.

The Window: You must press the button after the cue begins (or just as it flashes) but before the danger hits.

Too Early: Dirk ignores the command (and usually walks off a cliff).

Too Late: Dirk dies (skeletonized, burned, flattened, etc.).

Audio Cues: Listen closely! Thanks to the MSU-1 audio, many visual flashes are accompanied by a distinctive "Beep" or clear musical sting.
